Prevention Checklist:

Do’s:

1.Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
3.Think carefully before sharing personal information online.
4.Adjust privacy settings on your social media accounts.
5.Be respectful and responsible while posting or commenting.
6.Verify links and messages before clicking or responding.
7.Keep your devices updated with the latest security software.
8.Talk to someone you trust if you face online harassment or threats.

Financial/UPI Safety Tips:
9. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone.
10. Do not scan unknown QR codes or click on suspicious payment links.

Don’ts:
11. Do not respond to abusive or threatening messages.
12. Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.
13. Do not share sensitive information like bank details or passwords.
14. Do not post anything that can harm your reputation or others.

Reporting Channels:
15. Report cybercrime at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

16. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
